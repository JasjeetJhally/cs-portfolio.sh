Arduino Push Button

Arduino is electronic platform that allows for the building of interactive electric creations. The microcontroller board that is a part of the Arduino is a able to read inputs. Through the Arduino Programming Language, one can give a set of directions to the microcontroller board. For my Arduino project, I decided to create a button that, when pushed, can light up an LED light. The buttons are the input, while the lit LED light is the output. For this project, I used two buttons, and LED light, three resistors, and seven wires.
