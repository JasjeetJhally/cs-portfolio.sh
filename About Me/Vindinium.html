Vindinium

What is it?

Vindinium is a turn-based game with four players, also known as heros. The main objective of the game is to collect the most amount of gold in a set amount of total turns; in most cases, this is 1200 turns, with each players receiving 300 turns. In order to collect gold, players must take control of the numerous gold mines located all around the map. To obtain these mines, the player must defeat a goblin guarding the mine. Once defeated, the goblin will then defend the player’s mine from other players; the player receives one piece of gold per turn for every mine that they control. In addition to obtaining mines, the players can also attack other players. The survivor of an attack against another player receives control of all the mines that the defeated player had already obtained. A player is killed when their life points have all elapsed, and when killed, the player automatically respawns with all the gold that they previously had, but no control over the mines that they previously acquired. During the game, players can visit taverns that contain cream soda, and restore their life points for the price of a piece of gold. In the end, the hero with the most gold is deemed the winner of the match.
